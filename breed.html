The different species that live here

The Emperor penguin

© Getty/Independent

The Emperor penguin is a tall penguin at approximately 1.2 meters. This penguin species population is neither increasing or decreasing, it is stable. It lives for about 20 years and regularly swims at the speed of 10 km/t. It is a beautiful and slow penguin. It cannot fly.

The Chinstrap penguin

© Christian Aslund/Greenpeace/PA

The Chinstrap penguin is a average tall penguin at approximately 0.7 meters. This penguin species population is strongly increasing, it is far more than stable. It lives for about 15-20 years and regularly swims at the speed of 30 km/t. It is a beautiful and fast penguin. It cannot fly.

The Rockhopper penguin

© Gentoo Multimedia Ltd./Shutterstock

The Chinstrap penguin is a average tall penguin at approximately 0.7 meters. This penguin species population is strongly decreasing, it is not stable at all. It lives for about 13 years and regularly swims at the speed of 35 km/t. It is a beautiful and super-fast penguin. It cannot fly.

The Gentoo penguin

© Dalia and Giedrius/DG Wildlife

The Rockhopper penguin is a short penguin at approximately 0.5 meters. This penguin species population is strongly decreasing, it is not stable at all. It lives for about 10 years and regularly swims at the speed of 7 km/t. It is a beautiful and really cool penguin. It cannot fly.
