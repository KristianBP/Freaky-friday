Penguin Paradise Shelter

Penguin Paradise Shelter is a new company that owns multiple penguin shelters.
Our company was created due to how much we dislike how zoo animals are held
in zoos/prisons all over the world. We are strongly against animal cruelty and want
to remove all zoos not letting the animals live in their natural climate with natural surroundings.
Our favourite animal is penguins. Therefore our shelters are all nearby to McMurdo Station in Antarctica.
We plan and make flights for you so that is no worry, just head over to contact us! The shelters are massive
and we plan trips with a tour-guide if wanted, or you can go by yourself. We recommend going with a
tour-guide if you don't know how to operate a snowmobile or a so called "snow-car". You have to pay a deposit
if you choose to not go with a guide in case of any accidents.

Flight price: 200$ worldwide
Access to shelter per day: 50$
Access to snowmobile per day: 25$
Tour guide + snowmobile per day: 50$
Hotel + food per day: 25$
